ATLS 2100: Image

Project 01- Lightroom & Photoshop - Re-framing the Portrait

Prompt: Select a stanza of a poem or a verse of lyrics that speaks to you and who you are or who the person you're representing is. Represent each line of the poem or song by creating portraits of yourself or your subject that correspond to each lyric. How you define portraiture can be literal or abstract. Visually represent your subject using the text to guide you.

For this prompt I chose to reframe the song "Amazing Life" by Britt Nicole. This song talks about how amazing life is and I chose to represent this by showing all of the amazing places I've traveled.
